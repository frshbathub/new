Our Story

Bloom & Grow began with a simple passion: bringing the beauty of nature into every home and garden. Founded in 2015 by horticulture enthusiast Maria Thompson, our journey started in a small greenhouse with just a handful of plant varieties.

What started as a weekend hobby quickly blossomed into a thriving business as word spread about our healthy plants, fresh flowers, and personalized service.
